Welcome to Inhabit

Inhabit is a four day conference hosted by the New Zealand Institute of Architects, that runs from November 15 - 18. Inhabit aims to create a connection between us and our architecture. Its location in Christchurch city brings forwards ideas and theories of how we can we rebuild our city into a place of revival and inspiration. The conference starts off with local architects speaking about their opinions on the rebuild. Over the 4 days the conference builds up into a final discussion involving theories of the creation of positive and inspiring architecture. It is here you will discover the link between vitality and architecture.

"We shape our buildings; therefore they shape us" – Winston Churchill

Day 1

Boffa Miskell


Boffa Miskell is a New Zealand environmental planning and design consultary. They provide services in landscape architecture, planning, urban design, ecology and cultural heritage to costumers all over New Zealand. They are currently the leading team in the rebuild of Christchurch city after the earthquakes.

"Architecture should speak of time and place, but yearn for timelessness" – Frank Gehry

Day 2

Warren & Mahoney


Warren and Mahoney are an insight led architectural practice with clients and projects spanning across New Zealand. The company currently has over 100 employees specialising in areas such as architecture, workplace, master planning, urban design and sustainable design.

"Objects and places touch us through their beauty" – Alain de Botton

Day 3

Context Architects


Context is an architectural firm with offices in Auckland and Christchurch. They bring fresh design perspectives and a mid-sized team of 20 employees that provide you with dedicated work and a strong costumer to client relationship.

"Vitality is radiated from exceptional art and architecture" – Arthur Erickson
