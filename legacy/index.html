Inhabit

Inhabit is a conference I created for the final project of my degree. It aims to create a connection between us and our architecture. The idea focuses on the rebuild and revival of Christchurch city after the earthquakes. For the final outcome I create an A2 poster, a conference package which included tickets, lanyards, stickers and a magazine and a conference website.
